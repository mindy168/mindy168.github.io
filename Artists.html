Ariana Grande

At the young age of 26, this artist has already written a total of 5 albums that show off her stunning vocal range. Her latest, Thank You, Next, features hit singles "Thank You, Next" and "7 Rings", which also show Grande becoming more personal with her music.

Jessie J

Jessie J kicked off her career with her debut album, called Who Are You, featuring her hit single "Price Tag". However, after overcoming troubles with her record label, she made a comeback in 2018 with her album called R.O.S.E., which is a more introspective album where she shares more about her feelings with her audience.

Paramore

Paramore became a band in 2004, with Hayley Williams as lead singer. However, over the years, despite the different members leaving with multiple disputes with them, Williams still managed to keep the band together and released their newest album, called After Laughter.
