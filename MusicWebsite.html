Artists:

Ariana Grande

At the youngs age of 26, this young artist has already written a total of 5 albums that show off her stunning vocal range. Her latest, Thank You, Next, features hit singles "Thank You, Next" and "7 Rings", which represent Grande becoming more personal with her music.

To watch a video showcasing her vocals, click here!

Jessie J

Jessie J kicked off her career with her debut album, called Who Are You, featuring her hit single "Price Tag". However, after overcoming troubles with her record label, she made a comeback in 2018 with her album called R.O.S.E., which is a more introspective album where she talks about more personal ideas.

Here is a song I recommend from her most recent album, called "Think About That".

Paramore

Paramore became a band in 2004, with Hayley Williams as lead singer. However, over the years, despite the different members leaving with multiple disputes with them, Williams still managed to keep the band together and released their newest album, called After Laughter.

Click here to view their song, "Hard Times", which I really like!
